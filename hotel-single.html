Chitwan National Park

238 Rating

Price: Rs.25,000/-

Experience the wilderness of Nepal with our Chitwan Jungle Safari Tour. Explore the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Chitwan National Park, home to rare wildlife, vibrant Tharu culture, and breathtaking natural beauty. Perfect for families, nature lovers, and adventure seekers, this tour gives you the best of Nepal's wildlife and cultural heritage.

  • Inclusions
  • Accommodation in eco-lodges / resorts
  • All meals during the stay (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  • Guided jungle activities (safari, canoe, birdwatching, cultural program)
  • National park entry fees
  • Transfers to/from Chitwan
  • Exclusions
  • Personal expenses (drinks, souvenirs)
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips to guides & drivers

On this Chitwan tour, you will immerse yourself in the vibrant wilderness of Nepal's premier national park. Begin your adventure with thrilling jeep and elephant safaris, offering close encounters with rare wildlife such as rhinos, deer, and a variety of exotic birds. Glide along the serene Rapti River on a canoe, spotting waterfowl and enjoying the peaceful surroundings. Explore the dense jungle on guided walks led by experienced naturalists, who will share fascinating insights about the flora, fauna, and the delicate ecosystem of the park. Evenings are filled with cultural experiences, including traditional Tharu dance and music performances, giving you a glimpse into the rich local heritage. Along the way, savor authentic Nepali cuisine and relax in comfortable lodges that blend seamlessly with nature, creating an adventure that is both exhilarating and rejuvenating.
