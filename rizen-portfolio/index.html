My Best Works
Stardew Valley

A cozy, life-simulation RPG that invites you to restore your grandfather's neglected farm, grow crops, raise animals, mine, fish, and build relationships in a vibrant small town. Despite being crafted by a solo developer, Eric Barone, the game offers deep systems and lovingly detailed pixel visuals, from seasonal scenes to charming NPC animations. Its relaxing gameplay combined with a rich pixel aesthetic has made it a global indie sensation.

Pizza Tower

A frenetic 2D platformer inspired by Wario Land, starring the expressive Peppino. It combines hyper-energetic level design, fluid and dynamic animations, and an intentionally quirky 'cheap-cartoon' art style that brims with personality. Beyond its nostalgic references, Pizza Tower stands out for its tight controls, inventive humor, and visually chaotic brilliance.

Eastward

This narrative-driven action-adventure RPG unfolds in a dilapidated yet atmospheric world. You journey alongside a resourceful girl and a mysterious older man through decaying towns and eerie undergrounds. Eastward's pixel art is rich and expressive, its bustling NPCs and dark caverns evoke nostalgia and depth, reminiscent of EarthBound's emotive visuals.
